About Us

Welcome to Kids Campus Nursery and Primary School. Here’s a little about who we are and what we believe in.

School Motto

Learn, Play and Grow together.

School Mission

Developing excellent and affordable education geared towards developing a holistic child.

School Vision

Meeting the growth needs of a child.

School Values

  • God fearing: We honor each student’s uniqueness and foster a culture of mutual respect.
  • Transparency: Integrity and honesty guide all our actions and decisions.
  • Accountability: We believe in building strong partnerships with families and the community.
  • Result Oriented: We are committed to achieving excellence across academics, arts, and personal growth.
  • Patience: We are dedicated to nurturing excellence in academics, arts, and character development.

Our History

Founded in 2000, Kids Campus Nursery and Primary School has been committed to excellence in early childhood education for over two decades. Our journey began with a small class of 15 students and has grown to a vibrant community of learners from diverse backgrounds.

Our Educational Approach

We combine modern teaching techniques with traditional values to provide a balanced education that nurtures the whole child. Our curriculum includes a mix of academics, arts, and physical education to support intellectual, social, and emotional growth.
