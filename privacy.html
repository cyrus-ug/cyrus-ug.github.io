Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Kids Campus Nursery and Primary School collects, uses, and discloses your information when you visit our website and use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information from you in various ways, including when you:

  • Fill out a form on our site
  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • Interact with us via email, phone, or social media

How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To improve our website and services
  • To process your requests and inquiries
  • To send periodic emails regarding your order or other products and services
  • To respond to your comments and questions

Data Protection

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. These measures include encryption, firewalls, and secure server hosting.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties unless we provide users with advance notice. This does not include website hosting partners and other parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you and to ask that we correct any inaccuracies. You may also request the deletion of your personal data in certain circumstances.

Cookies

Our website may use "cookies" to enhance the user experience. Users can choose to set their web browser to refuse cookies or to alert them when cookies are being sent.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Kids Campus Nursery and Primary School may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address specified in your account or by placing a prominent notice on our site.
