Eligibility Criteria

We accept students from ages 3 to 12 for our Nursery and Primary School sections. Each applicant should meet age requirements as specified by the Ministry of Education.

Required Documents

  • Passport-sized Photos
  • Previous School Records (if applicable)
  • Medical Certificate
  • Completed Application Form
